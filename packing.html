What to Pack for Your Next Trip

Use this checklist to make sure you're ready for your next travel adventure:

Essential Documents
  • Passport
  • Visas
  • Tickets
  • Travel Insurance
  • Copies of Important Documents
Clothing
  • Weather-Appropriate Attire
  • Underwear and Socks
  • Sleepwear
  • Accessories (Hats, Scarves, etc.)
  • Comfortable Footwear
Tech Gear
  • Phone and Charger
  • Camera and Accessories
  • Adapters and Converters
  • Power Bank
  • Laptop/Tablet and Chargers
Toiletries
  • Toothbrush and Toothpaste
  • Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Deodorant
  • Skincare Products
  • Personal Medications
Miscellaneous
  • Travel Pillow
  • Reusable Water Bottle
  • Books/E-Reader
  • Snacks
  • First Aid Kit
